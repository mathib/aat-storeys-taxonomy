bouwlaagc back to ToC or Class ToC

IRI: https://w3id.org/con-tax/zones/aat-storeys#300002667

Horizontal stages or levels of a building positioned one above the other and having a habitable or usable room or set of rooms on each level. The term may also refer to horizontal divisions of the exteriors of buildings that typically, but not always, correspond to the levels of interior spaces; exterior indications of stories include tiers or rows of orders, columns, window mullions, or other features disposed horizontally one above another.

triforiumsc back to ToC or Class ToC

IRI: https://w3id.org/con-tax/zones/aat-storeys#300002678

Stories in basilican church interiors that correspond to the level between the vaulting and the aisle roof, or the vaulting of the tribune and the tribune roof; may or may not contain a small passage. Distinct from "tribunes (stories)," which always have substantial passages.
